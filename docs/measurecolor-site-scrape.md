## 16. News — MeasureColor Production 24.3 (2024-10-24)

- **URL:** https://measurecolor.com/2024/10/24/mc-24-3/
- **Breadcrumb:** Home › News › 2024/10/24

MeasureColor Production 24.3 introduces several enhancements designed to streamline press operations and improve workflow efficiency.

### Enhanced job information

Over 85% of MeasureColor users automatically generate jobs through their prepress workflow, meaning all print job details are established before reaching the press. Operators need direct access to this information for proper setup decisions. The latest version centralizes this data with a single-click interface. The job setup tab displays job metadata, color targets, tolerance settings, measuring conditions, and color bars. An additional job extra tab provides preset information and custom data fields, while the ink details tab reveals ink sequences, lab color data, and ∆E tolerance per ink.

### Automatic job setup for image module
